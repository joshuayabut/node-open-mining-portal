The Parties further acknowledge that in consideration for the contributions of the ZClassic Miners, each participating miner will receive 1.25 BTCP for each ZCL coin contributed and deposited into the Fund (the "Miner Reward”). The Miner Reward will be issued to the contributing ZClassic Miners, after the Fork Date, for each ZCL contributed to the Fund up to 50,000 ZCL or the aggregate amount of ZCL contributed by ZClassic Miners on the eve prior to the Fork Date, whichever event takes place sooner. The BTCP blockchain developers have developed a protocol on the blockchain that will code the fork such that these Miner Rewards are generated on the Fork Date and sent directly to the ZClassic Miners who contributed to the Fund pursuant to this Agreement.

The Parties further acknowledge that in consideration for the contributions of the ZClassic Miners, each participating miner will receive a minimum of 1.25 BTCP for each ZCL coin contributed and deposited into the Fund (the “Miner Reward”). The Miner Reward will be issued to the contributing ZClassic Miners, after the Fork Date, for each ZCL contributed to the Fund up to 50,000 ZCL or the aggregate amount of ZCL contributed by ZClassic Miners on the eve prior to the Fork Date, whichever event takes place sooner. The BTCP blockchain developers have developed a protocol on the blockchain that will code the fork such that these Miner Rewards are generated on the Fork Date and sent directly to the ZClassic Miners who contributed to the Fund pursuant to this Agreement.

Two or more members from the Fund shall also hold keys to the Electrum Wallet to further the BTCP Community goal of transparency and security.

The ZCL coins mined and contributed directly to the Fund will be strictly used for those expenses incurred in-connection with the Bitcoin Private fork. The BTCP Community is required to pay fees and expenses including, but not limited to, those fees incurred in having BTCP listed on multiple exchanges, fees paid to BTCP developers, marketing teams, administrators, and legal counsel. At the time of the drafting of this Agreement, the BTCP Community estimates the following breakdown with respect to the allocation of the Fund’s expenses: a. 40% of the Fund towards expenses incurred to have BTCP listed on multiple exchanges; b. 25% of the Fund towards remuneration of the BTCP Development Team; c. 20% of the Fund towards the General and Administrative tasks; and d. 15% of the Fund towards remuneration of the BTCP Marketing Team.

The Parties hereto fully acknowledge and understand that the date and time of the Fork Date of ZClassic is subject to change. The Parties further acknowledge that, in the unforeseeable event that the ZClassic fork does not occur (thus rendering Bitcoin Private nonexistent), any and all assets in the Fund that were contributed by ZClassic Miners ("Residual Assets”) will be remitted back to the ZClassic Miners. For purposes of this Article 5, "Residual Assets” shall be the total number of ZClassic coins contributed to the Fund by ZClassic Miners, less those ZCL coins used by the BTCP Community pursuant to the terms and conditions set forth herein. Any and all fees or costs related to the transaction and/or transfer of ZCL back to the ZClassic Miners will be incurred by the ZClassic Miners.

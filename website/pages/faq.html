FAQ


Why Does my ZCL/BTCP Balance seem inaccurate for the time I have been mining?

The current blocks have to confirm fully ( After 100 Confirmations ) Then payments are processed and your balance will update accordingly. Please see the Graph Stats Page and look at current blocks found, blocks must be fully confirmed before payment processing occurs.

Can I use an exchange address to mine with?

No - You will need to use an address that is in a wallet where you have control of the private key. Make sure you save this address and backup the private key, as you will need both to redeem your BTCP after the fork.

Can I use an T (transparent) address from Electrum or Eleos to mine and receive my BTCP when the time comes?

No - You will need to use an address that is in a wallet where you have control of the private key. Make sure you save this address and backup the private key, as you will need both to redeem your BTCP after the fork.

Can I use an T (transparent) address from Electrum or Eleos to mine and receive my BTCP when the time comes?

YES! - Keep hold of your wallet files / seed words / private keys from our supported wallets. where you have control of the private key. Make sure you save this address and backup the private key, as you will need both to redeem your BTCP after the fork.

Why do I keep getting an Unauthorized or Authorization failed error message?

You must to use a T address as the user in your mining program; you cannot use a Z address or an exchange address. Also make sure that the address is valid, as most authorization errors have been due to mistyping the address or case-sensitivity. Simply copy and paste whole T address for best results, and double-check your address. Your address can be in the format of ( T_address.workername ) for your miner.
